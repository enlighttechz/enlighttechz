Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services and process.

What services does Enlight Techz offer?

We provide a comprehensive suite of premium IT solutions including Custom Web Development, iOS & Android Mobile App Development, Enterprise Software Development, advanced SEO (Search Engine Optimization), AEO & GEO (AI and Generative Engine Optimization), customized CRM Solutions, and cutting-edge AI Agent Development tailored to elevate your business.

How can I request a quote or consultation?

You can request a free consultation through our website contact form, via email, or by calling our sales team directly. We'll deliver a customized solution and detailed quote after understanding your needs.

What industries do you serve?

Our solutions are tailored for diverse industries such as finance, healthcare, education, e-commerce, manufacturing, logistics, and tours and travels, ensuring compliance, security, and scalable infrastructure.

How do you handle data security and privacy?

We prioritize security using industry-standard encryption, secure cloud hosting, regular audits, and strict compliance with regulations like GDPR and local cybersecurity laws.

What is your support process?

We provide 24/7 technical support via phone, email, and remote assistance. Clients also have access to our knowledge base and ticketing system for prompt issue resolution.

Do you provide customized software solutions?

Yes, we specialize in custom software development tailored to your specific requirements, ensuring seamless integration with your existing systems and scalability for future growth.

How long does it take to implement IT solutions?

Timelines vary by complexity. Typically, small projects take a few weeks, while enterprise-scale solutions may require several months, with periodic updates provided throughout.

What are your pricing models?

We offer flexible pricing options including fixed-price projects, hourly rates, and subscription-based managed services, allowing you to choose a plan that fits your budget.
